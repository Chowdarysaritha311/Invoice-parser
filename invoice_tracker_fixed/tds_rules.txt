MATERIAL SUPPLY RULE:
If vendor supplies ALL materials needed for the work, it is a product purchase and TDS is NOT applicable.
TDS applies only when WE supply the materials and vendor provides labour or service only.

Examples:
- Printing where vendor supplies paper + ink = Product = No TDS
- Printing where we supply paper and vendor only prints = Service = 194C applies
- Civil work where vendor brings cement + sand + labour = Product = No TDS
- Catering where vendor brings food and cooks = Product = No TDS

HSN CODE GUIDE:
- HSN 48, 49 (paper, printed matter) = Goods = No TDS
- HSN 39 (plastics, vinyl, flex) = Goods = No TDS
- HSN 9988 (manufacturing on inputs owned by others) = Service = TDS applies
- HSN 9954 (construction) = check who supplies material

WHEN TDS APPLIES:

Section 194C - Contractor Payments
Applies to: transport, courier, security, manpower, housekeeping, labour only work
Rate: 1% for Individual or HUF, 2% for Company or Firm
No TDS if payment less than Rs 30,000 and annual total less than Rs 1,00,000

Section 194J - Professional Services
Applies to: consulting, legal, IT, software, accounting, design, training
Rate: 10% professional, 2% technical services
No TDS if annual payment less than Rs 30,000

NO TDS:
- Vendor supplies materials along with work
- Purchase of goods or products
- GST payments
- Reimbursements
- Government payments
